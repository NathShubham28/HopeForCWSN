About the Foundation

Mainstream educational center is the first educational center of Hope for children with special needs, located at Malviya Nagar, New Delhi.

It primarily, focuses on the overall development of the children with special needs and aims to make them independent. Mainstream educational center provides better understanding and learning experience to children with special abilities .Here, we provide special educators who are specialized in dealing children with special needs. All our Special educators are RCI registered and are well trained to apply different teaching strategies by bending the basics.

We do Assessments, prepare individual plans for every child with special needs and work on short and long term goals. We work on their abilities, strengths, needs. Special educators at Mainstream education not only focuses on their academics, but works on basic education and various sports & co-curricular activities to enhance their life skills. We provide IEP, (Individual Educational Program) in which our special educator works on individual children with special needs (1:1)at his/her level of comfort and mental strength. We prepare particular IEP file for each individual that helps us to maintain and analyse their records and abilities. Also, we provide different therapies depending on the individual and it includes ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis) that focuses on improving specific behaviors of the children with special abilities and adaptive learning skills like communication, social skills. We also provide vocational trainings to enhance their skills, entail unique techniques and tools to impart education. We provide them livelihood and make them competent for jobs.

Mainstream education center organises various counselling sessions for parents, family, friends and awareness campaign in various educational institutions. Quality of education depends on the environment. We provide them a healthy and a positive environment. This aids their social and emotional growth. Thus, we aim to make these special children with special needs physically, mentally, emotionally, socially and financially independent.
